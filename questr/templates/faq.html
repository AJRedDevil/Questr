What is Questr?

Questr is a crowdshipping - or a peer-to-peer shipping - company. Essentially it is a courier company, but with a different mindset from that of traditional courier companies.

How is Questr different from other courier services?

Unlike traditional courier companies like Canada Post or UPS or FedEx, Questr's shippers get packages and deliver them to the destination directly without the hassle of going to local facilities, saving much time and labour that leads to a higher shipping cost, by breaking down the centralized logistics and letting the crowd deliver the packages.

Is Questr safe?

Safety is Questr's top priority. By utilizing a multitude of security measures, Questr is guaranteed to be not only just as safe, but also even more trackable and traceable.

How does Questr verify its shippers?

Questr verifies its shippers in a number of ways, including credit card binding, driver's license binding, cell phone verification, etc.

How fast does Questr delivery its packages?

Once a shipper picks up a package, s/he does not need to go to a local sorting facilities but driving directly from the pickup locaiton to dropoff location. A package from Scarborough Town Centre to Square One Mall may only take fourty-five minutes if the traffic is reasonably good.

How much does a Questr shipment cost?

Questr offers three types of size options: backpack, car, and minivan, and they cost from $6 to $16 plus per-km fees.
At night or during weekends, shipping reates are slightly more expensive.

Backpack, car, minivan, what's the difference?

A backpack package can be a pile of documents or a takeout order. Each costs $6 + $1.7/km.
A car package can be 4-6 moving boxes, or anything that can fit in a normal car. Each costs $6 + $2/km.
A minivan package can be requested for moving purposes. Each costs $16 + $3.4/km.

Where is Questr currently available?

Starting from July 28th, 2014, Questr is first available in Greater Toronto Area.

How do I become a shipper?

Go to Contact Us and let us know you are interested. We will get back to you within 8 hours.

What kind of vehicle do I need in order to become a shipper?

A bike, a scooter, a car, anything that can get you around the city quickly.
