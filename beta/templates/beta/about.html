About.

More About Questr.

Founded in March of 2014 by two undergraduate students at the University of Western Ontario and based in Toronto, Questr is one of world's (and definitely Canada's) first peer-to-peer courier services. Questr believes in using the Internet way of thinking to improve the world and aims to make consumer delivery much cheaper, faster, and more economical.
Unlike traditional courier companies like Canada Post or UPS or FedEx, Questr breaks down the centralized logistics and lets its users deliver the packages. A shipper user gets packages and delivers them to the destination directly without the hassle of going to local facilities, saving much time and labour that leads to a higher shipping cost. At the same time, the shipper monetizes the extra space on their bike or in their car. By utilizing a multitude of security measures, such as credit card binding, photo ID verification, and a review system, each shipment is guaranteed to be just as safe and even more trackable and traceable.

How Questr Works.



Post a quest & Pick a shipper

3 minutes

Tell us where to pick up & drop off, and the size of the package. Questr will show you the lowest price. After submission, shippers around will be notified. A whole bunch of shippers will apply for the shipment, choose one of your own liking or let us do the job. After choosing a shipper, the deposit will be charged.

Pickup

5 ~ 10 minutes

The chosen shipper will call you and confirm the details. Then they'll come and pick up your package and start driving right away.

Dropoff

15 ~ 45 minutes

The shipper will drive your package undistractably and directly to the dropoff location. Nothing will stop them from shipping your package.

Rate your experience & Release the reward

30 seconds

Opinions matter. If you think your shipper was a good one. Let him and other people know since this is crucial for a community. After sending your review, the reward that has been depostied before will be released to the shipper.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Questr as safe as other companies?

Questr utilizes a multitude of security measures, such as credit card binding, photo ID verification, and a review system, to make sure that each shipment is just as safe and even more trackable and traceable. Oh, you can also choose a shipper of your own liking.

How much does Questr value safety?

Questr has a zero tolerance policy towards frauds and other unethical and illegal activities. If detected, a user, along their associated cell phone number, email address, social network account, credit card, and photo id, will be immediately and permanently banned and put on our blacklist.

How can I make sure if a user is credible?

Besides what Questr does automatically for you, you can also go to a shipper's profile and go over the reviews that have been provided by other users.

When will Questr be coming to T.O.?

An excellent service isn't built overnight. We are working hard with the team, and are expecting to send out our beta test invitations in late June of 2014.
