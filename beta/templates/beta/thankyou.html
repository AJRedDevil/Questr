What is Questr?

One of the world's first, Questr is a crowdshipping platform that provides cheap, fast, and economical shipments within Greater Toronto Area.
Unlike traditional courier companies like Canada Post or UPS, Questr's shippers deliver packages directly to the destination without the hassle of going to local facility, saving much time and labour.

About US

Questr is Canada's first crowdshipping platform and aims to provide cheaper, faster, and more economical shipping using the Internet way of thinking. The startup was founded in March of 2014 by two undergraduate students at the University of Western Ontario.
