What is Questr?

Founded in early 2014 by two undergraduates at the University of Western Ontario, Questr is a peer-to-peer stratup that provides cheap, fast, and economical shipments within Greater Toronto Area and is one of the first in the world that provides the service.
Unlike traditional courier companies like UPS, Questr uses decentralized logistics and lets its users deliver the packages. A shipper user delivers the packages directly to the destination without the hassle of going to local facility, saving much time and labour.

How can I trust a stranger?

Questr utilizes a multitude of security measures, such as credit card binding, photo ID verification, and a review system, to make sure that each shipment is just as safe and even more trackable and traceable. Oh, you can also choose a shipper of your liking.
Questr has a zero tolerance policy towards frauds and other immoral and illegal activities. If detected, a user, along their associated cell phone number, email address, social network account, credit card, and photo id, will be immediately and permanently put on our blacklist.

How can I make sure whether a user is credible?

Besides what Questr does automatically for you, you can also go to a shipper's profile and go over the reviews that have been provided by other users.
