What is Questr?

Questr is the new way to get your orders to you.


Questr helps you:

  • 1

  • Receive packages as fast as the most expensive
    shipping option for a fraction of the cost.

  • 2

  • Make money while you travel.

How does it work?

1. Find Something You Want

Megan, a Questr in Canada, is looking to buy some vegemite.
She checks online, and prices go for 600% of what vegemite would normally go for in Australia! (Not including shipping!)

2. Post A Quest

Megan posts a Quest, offering a reward for anyone travelling from Australia to Canada willing to bring her some vegemite.

3. Challenge Accepted

Jen is travelling from Australia to Canada, and still has plenty of space in her suitcase. She accepts the Quest to bring Megan her vegemite.

4. Meet Up

Megan is happy, because she received her package for a fraction of the cost and time. Jen is happy, because she made some money travelling.
