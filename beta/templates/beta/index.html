What is Questr?

Questr is a crowdshipping platform that provides cheap, fast shipments within GTA.
Unlike other courier companies, Questr's shippers deliver packages directly to the destination without the hassle of going to local facilities.

Cheaper

Starting from only $6, Questr offers the cheapest shipping options available in the entire Canada.

Faster

Less than 1 hour from STC to Square One. Decentralized logistics makes shipping lightning fast.

Available 24/7

Streamlined experience

Shipping things has never been so easy like this.

1. Request & Pick

3 minutes

Tell us where to pick up & drop off, and the size of the package. Shippers around you will be notified and apply for the shipment, choose one of your own liking. After choosing a shipper, a deposit will be charged.

2. Pickup

5 ~ 10 minutes

The chosen shipper will call and confirm the details, then they'll come and pick up the package and start driving right away.

3. Dropoff

15 ~ 90 minutes

The shipper will drive the package undistractably and directly to the dropoff location. Nothing will stop them from shipping your package.

4. Finish & Rate

30 seconds

Opinions matter. If you think your shipper was a good one, let them and other people know since this is crucial for a community. After submitting your review, the deposit will be released to the shipper.

A few more details

Is Questr as safe as other shipping companies?

Questr utilizes a multitude of security measures, including credit card binding, photo ID verification, and a review system, to make sure that each shipment is just as safe and even more trackable and traceable. Oh, you can also choose a shipper of your own liking.

How much does Questr value ethics and safety?

Questr has a zero tolerance policy towards frauds and other unethical and illegal activities. If detected, a user, along their associated cell phone number, email address, social network account, credit card, and photo id, will be immediately and permanently banned and put on our blacklist.

How can I make sure whether a shipper is credible?

Besides what Questr does automatically for you, you can also go to a shipper's profile and go over the reviews that have been provided by other users.
